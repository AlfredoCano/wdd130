Splash Down Rafting

Our purpose is to provide an unforgettable adventure and connect people with the natural beauty and excitement of the great outdoors through safe and enjoyable rafting experiences. Our mission is to deliver exceptional service, create lifelong memories, and promote a love for nature and the environment through our guided rafting trips. At our company, we believe that adventure is essential to the human spirit, and that through experiencing the beauty and power of nature, we can gain a deeper appreciation for the world around us. We believe in safety first, and strive to create an environment of trust and respect among our guests, employees, and partners.

History

Our rafting company was founded in 2023 by Alfredo Cano, who had a passion for outdoor adventure and a love for the natural beauty of the south lakes. Starting with just a few rafts and a small team of guides, we began offering guided rafting trips down the "blue river" and quickly gained a reputation for providing safe, fun, and memorable experiences. Over the years, our company has grown and expanded, adding new rivers and destinations to our offerings and increasing our fleet of rafts and equipment. We have also expanded our team of guides and staff, hiring experienced professionals who share our passion for outdoor adventure and commitment to safety and service.
